BACKGROUND

A simple keyboard simulator will allow you to learn how to quickly type in English.
Key presses are accompanied by animation on the screen.
This simulator is indispensable for novice computer users,
helps you memorize the positions of letters on the keyboard,
and also extremely useful for children!

Rules:

- letters fall from the top of the screen in random places
- initial bonus - 10 points
- you need 100 points to win!
- when you press the right key, points are added, if the letter "breaks" - points are deducted.
- if the number of points is less than 2, you will lose...
- return to the menu by pressing the Escape key.


Press Escape to return to the main menu now!
